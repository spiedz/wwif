## üó∫Ô∏è Top 5 Must-Visit Filming Locations in Sofia

Step onto the real-life sets of your favorite films! These iconic Sofia landmarks have played pivotal roles in numerous Hollywood productions.

### 1. **Alexander Nevsky Cathedral** - Majestic Landmark

<img src="https://images.pexels.com/photos/28915257/pexels-photo-28915257.jpeg?cs=srgb&dl=pexels-pham-ngoc-anh-170983008-28915257.jpg&fm=jpg" alt="Alexander Nevsky Cathedral in Sofia, Bulgaria, a grand Eastern Orthodox cathedral with gold domes against a blue sky." style="width: 100%; height: auto; border-radius: 8px; margin: 15px 0;" />

**What You'll See:** One of the largest Eastern Orthodox cathedrals in the world, with impressive gold-plated domes and intricate architecture.
**Filming Connection:** While specific interior scenes from films are rarely disclosed for this sacred site, its grand exterior and surrounding square have reportedly been used for establishing shots or as a striking background in films like [*The Expendables 2*](/films/where-was-the-expendables-2-filmed) and [*London Has Fallen*](/films/where-was-london-has-fallen-filmed) to convey a European capital atmosphere.
**Best Photo Spots:** The square directly in front of the cathedral for wide-angle shots, or from the surrounding gardens to capture its unique domes.
**Access:** Free entry to the main cathedral nave; a small fee for the crypt and museum. Open daily from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
**Recognisable from:** Its distinctive golden domes and Byzantine revival architecture.

### 2. **Rotunda of St. George** - Ancient Hidden Gem

<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/60/StGeorgeRotundaSofia.JPG" alt="The ancient Rotunda of St. George in Sofia, Bulgaria, a brick church nestled amidst modern buildings and Roman ruins." style="width: 100%; height: auto; border-radius: 8px; margin: 15px 0;" />

**What You'll See:** Sofia's oldest preserved building, a 4th-century brick rotunda nestled within the courtyard of the Presidency and Sheraton Hotel amidst Roman ruins.
**Filming Connection:** Its unique historical context and secluded location make it a versatile backdrop. Reportedly used for atmospheric scenes in films needing a touch of ancient European history. Its hidden nature can be ideal for clandestine meetings or historical flashbacks in productions like those that require a timeless architectural presence.
**Best Photo Spots:** From within the courtyard, capturing the contrast between the ancient brickwork and modern surroundings.
**Access:** Free entry. Accessible year-round. It's often illuminated at night, offering a different photographic opportunity.

### 3. **Vitosha Mountain** - Dramatic Natural Backdrop

<img src="https://media.istockphoto.com/id/1134231744/photo/view-to-the-sofia-city-at-dusk-view-from-the-kopitoto-hill-vitosha-mountain-bulgaria.jpg?s=612x612&w=0&k=20&c=WAge1zjh9bVzIGIHZptx2FOowctgtX6RurvMy7hYco=" alt="A panoramic view of Vitosha Mountain overlooking Sofia, Bulgaria, with the city lights in the foreground at dusk." style="width: 100%; height: auto; border-radius: 8px; margin: 15px 0;" />

**What You'll See:** Sofia's constant companion, a majestic mountain offering panoramic city views, ski slopes, hiking trails, and dense forests.
**Filming Connection:** Vitosha's diverse landscapes make it an ideal stand-in for rugged wilderness or remote locations. It has been reportedly used for outdoor sequences, including chase scenes or training montages in action films like [*Rambo: Last Blood*](/films/where-was-rambo-last-blood-filmed) and parts of *The Expendables* series, offering a natural contrast to the urban settings.
**Best Photo Spots:** From Kopitoto (The Hoof) for sweeping city views, or along one of its many hiking trails for scenic shots.
**Access:** Easily accessible by public transport (bus 66, 93, or mini-buses) or taxi. Ski resorts operate in winter. Hiking trails are available year-round.

### 4. **Ivan Vazov National Theatre** - Elegant European Stage

<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/16/IvanVazov_National_Theatre_7.jpg" alt="The Ivan Vazov National Theatre in Sofia, Bulgaria, a grand neoclassical building with a vibrant green park and fountain in front." style="width: 100%; height: auto; border-radius: 8px; margin: 15px 0;" />

**What You'll See:** Bulgaria's oldest and most authoritative theatre, a stunning neoclassical building with a beautiful green park and fountain in front.
**Filming Connection:** Its elegant facade and central location have made it a popular choice for scenes requiring a grand European setting. Reportedly featured in establishing shots or as a backdrop for street scenes in films like [*The Hitman's Bodyguard*](/films/where-was-the-hitmans-bodyguard-filmed) and [*London Has Fallen*](/films/where-was-london-has-fallen-filmed), often doubling for Parisian or London surroundings.
**Best Photo Spots:** From across the park, capturing the entire building with the fountain, or close-ups of its intricate architectural details.
**Access:** Located in the city center, easily accessible by foot or metro (Serdika Station).

### 5. **Vitosha Boulevard** - Bustling Urban Hub

<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/b7/Boulevard_Vitosha_at_night%2C_Sofia_PD_2012_13.jpg" alt="Vitosha Boulevard in Sofia, Bulgaria, bustling with people at night, illuminated by streetlights and shop windows." style="width: 100%; height: auto; border-radius: 8px; margin: 15px 0;" />

**What You'll See:** Sofia's main shopping street, a lively pedestrian zone filled with shops, cafes, and restaurants, offering views of Vitosha Mountain.
**Filming Connection:** Its vibrant atmosphere and characteristic architecture make it ideal for urban scenes. Reportedly used for street walk-throughs, surveillance scenes, or establishing shots depicting a busy European city in productions like [*The Hitman's Bodyguard*](/films/where-was-the-hitmans-bodyguard-filmed) and others requiring a bustling city center.
**Best Photo Spots:** Along the boulevard at night for illuminated shop windows, or capturing the mountain view at the southern end.
**Access:** Easily accessible from any part of the city center.
