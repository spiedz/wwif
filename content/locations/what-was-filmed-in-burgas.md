- **Wide-angle lens:** Essential for capturing the vastness of the Sea Garden, beaches, and cityscapes.

- **Overcast Days:** Can offer diffused, soft light, excellent for intimate street photography or details on buildings.

### **Respectful Photography:**
- Always be aware of your surroundings, especially in busy areas like the Port.
- When photographing locals, a smile and a gesture or verbal request for permission is always appreciated.

- **Nodding & Shaking Head:** Bulgarians confusingly use a nod for "no" and a shake for "yes." This can take some getting used to!

- **Aqua Calidae (Therma):** Visit the ancient Roman baths and thermal springs, located just outside the city. A historically rich and relaxing escape.
- **Excursion to Burgas Lakes:** Beyond Poda, Burgas is surrounded by three large lakes (Mandrensko, Burgasko, Atanasovsko) which are important bird habitats. Rent a bike and explore the paths around them for a peaceful day out.
- **The Flora Exhibition Center:** Located within the Sea Garden, it hosts beautiful flower exhibitions throughout the year.
