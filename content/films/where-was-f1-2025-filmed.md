# Exploring the Filming Locations of *F1* (2025)

The adrenaline-fueled world of Formula 1 comes to life in *F1* (2025), directed by Joseph Kosinski and starring Brad Pitt as a veteran driver making a dramatic return to the sport. The film's authenticity owes everything to its bold filming approach: real tracks, real races, and real cars.

> üìç Filming took place during actual race weekends at tracks like **Silverstone**, **Hungaroring**, **Spa**, **Monza**, and more ‚Äî many of which have also appeared in [Where Was *Rush* Filmed?](/films/where-was-rush-filmed) and [Where Was *Grand Prix* Filmed?](/films/where-was-grand-prix-filmed).

## Introduction to *F1* (2025)

Produced in partnership with Formula One and Apple Original Films, *F1* tells the story of a retired driver (played by Pitt) returning to the grid to mentor a rising star while competing for his own shot at redemption. With unprecedented access to real-world F1 races, the production crew embedded itself into the heart of the action‚Äîmaking this one of the most immersive motorsports films ever made.

## Major Filming Locations

### 1. Silverstone Circuit, United Kingdom

![Silverstone Circuit](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3e/Silverstone_Circuit_2011.jpg)

#### The Location

Silverstone is the historic home of British motorsport and one of the fastest circuits in the world. Known for its sweeping turns and enthusiastic fans, it was the perfect backdrop for Pitt's character's dramatic debut.

#### In the Film

Many of the key race sequences were filmed during the 2023 British Grand Prix, with Pitt‚Äôs car racing on track between actual F1 sessions.

#### Visitor Information

- **Address:** Towcester NN12 8TN, UK  
- **Hours:** Event dependent  
- **Admission:** Tickets vary by event  
- **Website:** [silverstone.co.uk](https://www.silverstone.co.uk)

### 2. Hungaroring, Hungary

![Hungaroring](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/Hungaroring_circuit.jpg)

Set in the hills near Budapest, Hungaroring‚Äôs tight and twisty layout provided the perfect contrast to Silverstone‚Äôs fast corners. In the film, this location was used to highlight technical skill and driver strategy.

### 3. Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps, Belgium

![Spa-Francorchamps](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1e/Spa-Francorchamps_2011.jpg)

Known for its unpredictable weather and legendary Eau Rouge corner, Spa was filmed in rainy conditions, intensifying one of the movie's most dramatic crashes.
